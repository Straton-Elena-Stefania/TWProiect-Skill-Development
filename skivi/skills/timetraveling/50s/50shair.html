Hairstyling

Vintage Pinup Inspired Hairstyle Tutorial

It’s an era that is known to be notoriously glamorous – red lips, flowing skirts and curled silky hair. Add a bold edge to your outfit by re-creating this vintage look with our retro chic pinup hair tutorial.

You will need

Pintail Hair Comb
Hair Sectioning Grip Clips
TONI&GUY Extreme Style Creation Hairspray
1

Side Parting.

Part your hair to the side using a pintail comb. Brush through to untangle knots and flatten.

2

Section your Hair.

Section your hair into front and back sections. Clip the back in place and focus on the front for now.

3

Curl.

Take a section of the front layer and curl facing away from your face.

4

Pin in Place.

Wrap the hair around your fingers to create a loop and pin into place. Repeat this with all sections of your front layer.

5

Section the back.

With the back layer of hair, section again into two to create top and bottom layers.

6

Repeat Process.

Repeat what you have done with the front sections with the remaining top and bottom sections. Curl, roll and pin. Hold

7

Fix.

Once all your sections are pinned into place, fix with hairspray to hold the curls and allow the heat to cool.

8

Release the curls.

Once your curls have set, unravel them starting from the back. Be sure to leave the front curls in.

9

Brush through.

Brush through the loose curls to detangle and create a wavy look.

10

Front Curls.

Now it is time to unravel your front curls. Try to still keep the front and back curls separate.

11

Backcomb.

Club the front curls together and backcomb them together to merge and create volume. To neaten, you can smooth them down at the front.

12

Create your Roll.

With the newly backcombed front section of hair, wrap around your fingers to create a victory roll. Pin this into place at the top of your head.

13

Repeat.

Repeat this process: Backcomb, neaten, roll and pin. Fix the look with hairspray to finish. Done!
