Festive Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette, sliced into 24 pieces
  • 6 oz low fat cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup pesto
  • 1 cup fair trade cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup raw cranberries, chopped
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup fair trade honey
  • 3/4 cup pomegranate arils
  • 1 Tbsp orange zest

    • ü•ñ

    Preparation Instructions

    1 Lightly toast slices of baguette in the oven. Put the oven on broil and closely watch the slices. After about 2 minutes, flip the pieces over and let them broil again.

    2 Remove toast slices from oven and let cool. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on each piece of bread.

    3 On half the slices, spread about a teaspoon of pesto over the cream cheese

    4 Slice your cherry tomatoes in half and toss in olive oil. If desired, quickly heat them over medium heat in a sauce pan to lightly cook. Spoon tomatoes over your pesto layer. Finish with a sprinkle of pepper and/or a drizzle of balsamic vinegar.

    5 On the remaining slices of bread with cream cheese, prepare your cranberry-pomegranate relish. Place your cranberries, orange juice and honey in a blender or food processor. Run the food processor a few times so everything is chopped, but not fully blended. Remove from food processor and stir in pomegranate arils.

    6 Spoon the relish over the cream cheese layer and top with orange zest.

    Raspberry Hand Pies

    Ingredients:

    1 For the crust

  • 2 pie crusts, ready-made or homemade
  • 1 large egg, beaten

    • 2 For the filling

  • 2 pkg (6 oz each) Driscoll's fair trade raspberries
  • 1/4 cup fair trade granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

    • ü•ß

    Preparation Instructions

    1 Preheat oven to 375¬∞F. Line cookie sheet with parchment.

    2 On a lightly floured surface, unroll pie crusts and cut 20 round circles of dough, each 3¬Ω inches. Reroll scraps as necessary. Place dough circles on parchment lined cookie sheet.

    3 Stir together raspberries, sugar, cornstarch, and vanilla until berries just begin to release their juices and mixture is evenly blended.

    4 Brush half of dough circles with egg and top with an even amount of raspberry mixture. Use small cookie cutters to cut shapes out of remaining dough circles then place on top of dough circles with raspberry filling and press firmly around edges to seal (or seal with fork). Brush tops of hand pies with remaining egg and bake about 20 minutes or until pies are golden. Let cool slightly before serving or cool completely.

    Vegan Pumpkin Sage Gnocchi

    Ingredients

  • 1 package gnocchi
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1/2 cup raw cashews, soaked for 4 hours or overnight
  • 1/2 cup Fair Trade Certified cashew milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 4 sage leaves, plus more to garnish
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp non-dairy cream

    • ü•ß

    Preparation Instructions

    1 Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and saut√© until tender, about 5‚Äì7 minutes.

    2 Transfer sauteed garlic and onion to a high-speed blender. Add soaked raw cashews, cashew plant-based beverage, vegetable broth, pumpkin puree, four sage leaves, and sea salt and pepper. Blend on highest speed until mixture is smooth and creamy. Transfer pumpkin sauce to a pot and warm on the stove.

    3 While pumpkin sauce warms, cook gnocchi according to package directions.

    4 Once gnocchi is cooked, divide across four bowls and cover with pumpkin sauce. Garnish with sage leaves, drizzle with coconut cream, and enjoy.

    Nut Butter Confetti Balls

    Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Fair Trade Certified unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 1/2 cup pitted dates
  • 1/2 cup macadamia nuts
  • 1/3 cup (heaping) quick oats
  • 1+1/2 Tbsp nut butter of choice
  • 1 Tbsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 Tbsp Fair Trade Certified honey
  • 1+1/2 Tbsp colored sprinkles

    • ü•Æ

    Preparation Instructions

    1 Add coconut to food processor and process until fine. Add dates and process until they are broken up into small bits. Leave in food processor.

    2 In a small saucepan over low heat, combine nut butter, vanilla extract, and honey. Stir until well combined and warmed. Remove from heat.

    3 Add nut butter mixture, macadamia nuts, and oats to food processor. Pulse until ingredients are well-combined and a dough forms. Add sprinkles and pulse a few more times to incorporate.

    4 Remove dough from processor and roll into one-inch balls. Serve immediately, storing leftovers in an air-tight container.
